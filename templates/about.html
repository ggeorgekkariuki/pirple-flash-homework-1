The History of Us

Consider this an autobiography of a man I have known all my life. A mysterious man really, an enigma of sorts. The kind of man who has gone over mountains, but doesn't know it. A strange man, who has urges and desires, but most times has the strength to deny himself. Sometimes he conquers, and other times he concurs. He is Me.
